## AMBIGUITIES

- DEM/Crow's Nest bounds conflict: the prescribed Crow's Nest coordinate
  (45.42, -107.17) is south of the prescribed DEM bound (45.47). The DEM south bound
  uses the sourced 45.42 value so the verbatim landmark and blocking bounds test can
  coexist. The Crow's Nest landmark, both scout starts, its checkpoint, and two
  observations retain the sourced coordinate.
- Landmark confidence is not supplied in the §D landmark table for `divide`,
  `lone-tipi`, `reno-skirmish-line`, `timber`, `retreat-crossing`, `cedar-coulee`,
  `nye-cartwright-ridge`, `calhoun-coulee`, `deep-ravine`, and
  `sharpshooter-ridge`. APPROX/inference rows are LOW; exact named/marker rows without
  a checkpoint confidence are MEDIUM. Every affected provenance note is flagged.
- Placeholder polygon dimensions are absent for `timber-loop`, `village-strip`, and
  `deep-ravine-cover`. Their sourced centers/endpoints are repeated to make honest
  zero-area placeholders pending O6 instead of inventing offsets.
- Research §H has no behavioral-evidence row for `mcdougall`, `sitting-bull`, or
  `hump`. Their provenance uses the available §A/§B evidence verbatim and is flagged
  for review.
- `pack-train` has no sourced weapon mix or tactics profile. It uses an empty mix and
  the required-field `us-cav-doctrine-1876` placeholder.
- The listed US companies + pack train + two scout detachments total 15, while the
  blocking count requires 16 US units. Research §A's `civilians-interpreters` row is
  the 16th record, but the schema has no matching kind and the inputs do not normalize
  its personal arms, ammunition, mounted state, morale, tactics, formation, or start
  position. Its provenance identifies every placeholder.
- Scout ammunition quantities and formations are not supplied. `arikara-scouts` and
  `crow-scouts` use empty ammunition maps and flagged `DISPERSED` placeholders.
- `blackfeet-santee-pool` is located only as “north-center.” It uses the sourced
  village north-end coordinate as a flagged placeholder.
- Per-circle noncombatant population shares are absent. `hunkpapa-camp`,
  `oglala-camp`, `minneconjou-camp`, `sans-arc-camp`, `mixed-north-camp`, and
  `cheyenne-camp` use 0 as an explicit unresolved numeric sentinel rather than
  inventing a distribution of the ~7,000 total. Camp polygon dimensions and a
  noncombatant tactics profile are also absent; all seven noncombatant records use
  repeated sourced centers (where polygons apply) and a flagged required-field
  tactics placeholder. `mixed-north-camp` additionally lacks a numeric center.
- Order geometry is not numerically supplied for `benteen-oblique`, `packs-follow`,
  `kanipe-msg`, `martini-msg`, or `crazy-horse-sweep`. Their objectives omit unsourced
  coordinates while retaining the sourced descriptions in text/provenance.
- Checkpoint rows name collective actors while `Checkpoint.unitId` accepts one unit.
  Representatives are explicitly flagged: Scouts→`crow-scouts`; Reno battalion
  rows→`co-a`; Yates E/F→`co-f`; Right wing→`co-l`; Custer→`co-f`. Keogh→`co-i` and
  Weir→`co-d` follow their explicit leader attachments.
- Observation rows likewise name collectives. The dawn Scouts observation uses
  `crow-scouts`; Village→Reno uses `hunkpapa-camp`→`co-a`. These mappings are flagged.
- `v-mtc-crossing` supplies a 150 m tolerance but no checkpoint confidence or
  `toleranceMinutes`; it inherits 25 minutes from the base MEDIUM Ford B checkpoint
  rule and represents E/F with `co-f`.
- The v0.1.1 patch contract cannot fully express four required variants:
  `v-organized-last-stand` cannot add a tactics profile or change calibration end
  states; `v-c-company-split` cannot add `co-c-det`; `v-deep-ravine-ssl` has no base
  E-company Deep Ravine checkpoint or terminal minute to modify; and
  `v-benteen-prompt` cannot modify Benteen's leader delay. Expressible portions are
  encoded; omitted portions remain verbatim in descriptions and flagged provenance.
- Coalition casualty figures are side-wide, but calibration accepts unit keys only.
  The exact 31/60/300 killed and 100/160/200 wounded spreads are stored under
  `hunkpapa-pool` as a flagged aggregate and are not distributed without evidence.

## Deviations

- DEM south bound is 45.42 rather than 45.47: required to retain the sourced Crow's
  Nest coordinate while satisfying the blocking bounds test.
- Cover and camp polygon placeholders are zero-area rather than invented rectangles:
  the inputs prescribe centers but no dimensions.
- The four structurally unsupported variant operations remain descriptive/partial:
  only the three authorized schema amendments were applied.
- The 16th US unit uses the research civilian/interpreter row with flagged required-
  field placeholders: needed to satisfy the explicit blocking count without creating
  an unsourced historical group.
- Six noncombatant camp strengths use flagged zero sentinels: the numeric schema cannot
  represent unresolved values and the sources do not permit a population allocation.
- Coalition casualties are keyed as a flagged aggregate rather than silently divided:
  the schema lacks a side-level casualty target.
- `package-lock.json` was added although not listed in the layout: it is the standard
  reproducible output of the authorized `npm install`.
